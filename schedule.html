Schedule

The plan

Friday October 23, 2020

Setting Up • 4:00 PM

Set up will begin at 4:00 PM at Ampersea. Feel free to join us early if you'd like to help set up or just meet for the ceremony if you'd prefer.

Wedding Ceremony • 5:00 PM

The wedding ceremony will take place at a restaurant in Fells Point called Ampersea, at 1417 Thames Street, Baltimore, Maryland.

We suggest arriving about 20-30 minutes prior to the start of the ceremony in order to find parking in enough time. The parking lot directly next to Ampersea is only open to the public after 5:00 PM, so you'll want to park at the 1530 Thames Street garage instead.

Photography

Immediately following the ceremony, the bridal party will be taking photos around the harbor for about 30-60 minutes.

Open Bar • 5:30 PM

While photos are being taken, Ampersea will start to set up the space for dinner. During this time, everyone will be moved to the outdoor lounge area where they can take advantage of outdoor games, sign the guest book, and grab drinks at the bar.

Dinner & Party • 6:30 PM

Once everything is set up, we'll be having a buffet-style dinner. Feel free to go up for seconds or grab another drink from the bar at any time.

Following dinner, we'll do toasts, first dances and parents' dances, and then we'll do the traditional cake cutting. After cake, the rest of the night is up in the air. Chat, drink, eat more cake, dance, and have a good time.

Send Off • 9:00 PM

At the end of the night, we'll do an official send-off with sparklers, then guests are free to help break down or head home.
